How Oversight works

Three steps. The only app installs on your child's device — never on yours.

1

Create your account

Sign up on this website and set a device-protection password that only you know.

2

Set up the child's device

On your child's Android phone or tablet, open the setup link to install the Oversight app. iPhone & iPad support is coming soon.

3

Manage from anywhere

Log in to your dashboard from any browser to change what's blocked. Updates reach the device automatically.

Built for every device

Strong, tamper-resistant protection on the two platforms kids actually use.

iPhone & iPad Coming soon

Support for Apple devices is coming soon. Full protection on iPhone & iPad needs a more advanced setup (like supervising the device from a Mac) — we're working on making it simple. Android is available today.

Android Available now

Installs the Oversight guardian app as a device administrator. It filters web content, blocks apps, and enforces downtime and time limits — and can't be uninstalled without your password.
